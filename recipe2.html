Sunkissed Chicken with Rice & Salad




Ingredients

  • 1 kg of chicken fillets, I slice them in the middle .
  • 1 packet of feta cheese
  • 2 jars of sun-dried tomatoes with herbs (drain out oil )
  • 2 shallots
  • 3 cups cr√®me fraiche
  • 250ml cream
  • 2.5 tablespoons Japanese soy (Tamari)
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • salt & pepper


Show recipe in my native language



Do this

  1. Mix creme fraiche, cream, white wine,salt and pepper and let it sit for an hour in the fridge.

  2. Take chicken put it in a pan and scramble feta cheese on top of it.

  3. Fry in pan the shallot onions, garlic and add sun dried tomatoes once cooked a lil, and then add the liquid from the fridge and pour into oven pan where the chicken is. And Put the oven on 230degress for 30-40mins

  4. Serve with boiled rice and salad of choice
